The Spirit of Christmas



Welcome to Santa's Favourite website, based on a best-selling image called "The Spirit of Christmas."

We are an organization that tries to make a difference. When you purchase one of these products, the retail portion of each sale will be used to purchase high quality President's Choice Organics. Not a single dime will be used from the retail portion for administrative costs. The PC Organics will be delivered to food banks across Canada and America by an organized group of volunteers, to support our communities and environment.

The care for a group begins with each person. Show someone you're thinking of them and their well being. Send them Santa's favourite gifts.

Thanks for being part of the spirit.
